Content Writing Course

Course Summary: Level 1 Content Writing


This four-week beginner content writing course provides a comprehensive introduction to writing for various platforms.
Students will learn foundational writing skills, including grammar, vocabulary, structure, and style. The curriculum focuses on practical application through exercises and a final project.

Key topics covered:

Understanding content writing and its purpose
Basic writing structure and techniques
Grammar and vocabulary essentials
Creating engaging content for blogs and social media
Research and idea generation
Editing and proofreading

Assessment:

Students will be evaluated based on weekly writing assignments and a final content writing project.
