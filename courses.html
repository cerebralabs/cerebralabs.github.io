Content Writing Course

This Content Writing Course provides a comprehensive introduction to writing for various platforms. Students will learn foundational writing skills, including grammar, vocabulary, structure, and style. The curriculum focuses on practical application through exercises and a final project.

  • Understanding content writing and its purpose
  • Basic writing structure and techniques
  • Grammar and vocabulary essentials
  • Creating engaging content for blogs and social media
  • Research and idea generation
  • Editing and proofreading

Canva Course

It is a month long course designed to teach beginners the basics of using Canva. The course is one hour long and is scheduled for Sundays.

  • The first week covers the basics of Canva, including the interface, tools, and elements. It also introduces basic design principles like color theory, typography, and balance.
  • In the second week, students learn how to work with templates and create simple designs, especially for social media.
  • The third week explores Canva's free features, such as free elements and filters, and applies these to design marketing materials.
  • Finally, the fourth week involves a practical project where students apply the skills learned throughout the course.
